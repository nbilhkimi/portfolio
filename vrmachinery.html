Precision Simulation

VRMACHINERY is a cutting-edge virtual reality platform designed for high-risk industrial training. Unlike traditional methods, our simulation places operators inside hyper-realistic construction environments where they can master heavy machinery—from excavators to industrial saws—without physical risk. The system focuses on mechanical accuracy, situational awareness, and safety protocol adherence. By integrating real-world physics and modular equipment logic, VRMACHINERY provides a scalable solution for workforce development in civil engineering, forestry, and manufacturing. Every interaction is tracked, providing supervisors with data-driven insights into trainee performance and hazard response times.

Engineering Pipeline

01. Industrial Asset Modeling

CAD-to-VR workflow using Blender to ensure millimeter-perfect mechanical replicas. Focus on PBR texturing for realistic metallic and weathered surfaces.

02. Site Integration

Utilizing Unity URP for stable performance on standalone XR hardware. Custom shaders simulate atmospheric dust, metallic glare, and site-specific lighting.

03. Smart Safety Assistant

Integrated AI logic acts as a virtual foreman, providing real-time feedback on safety violations and operational efficiency based on site data.

04. Kinetic Programming

Advanced C# event-driven architecture designed to handle complex kinematics and multi-part machinery interactions seamlessly.
